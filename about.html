Brawl Stars AI Coach

Real-time AI coaching powered by Gemini. Get live voice callouts, threat analysis, and strategic advice while you play.

Why This Exists

Chess.com recently expanded their AI team and hired GM Vinay Bhat as a director to develop a coach that "knows what you've been doing, what are some of your strengths, weaknesses, patterns. It knows how to encourage you, sort of lean into positive reinforcement." Their vision: AI that continuously opens up a complex game to more and more players.

Brawl Stars has 10M+ active players and has gotten significantly more complex over 6+ years. Yet there are very few free resources to help players improve. This project applies that same AI coaching philosophy to a fast-paced mobile game -- giving every player access to a real-time coach that understands matchups, threats, and mode-specific strategy.

How It Works

1

Screen Capture

Share your game screen through the browser. Frames are captured at 2fps and streamed to the server via WebSocket for real-time analysis.

2

Loading Screen Detection

OpenCV analyzes each frame using color histogram heuristics -- detecting the distinctive blue (ally) and red (enemy) panels of the pre-match loading screen.

3

Roster & Mode Classification

Gemini's vision model identifies all 6 brawlers and the game mode from the loading screen. Each brawler's role, threat level, and weaknesses are loaded from a curated database.

4

Live Voice Coaching

Gemini 3.1 Flash Live receives game frames alongside a full context prompt with team compositions and mode strategy. It streams back real-time audio coaching callouts -- short, punchy advice focused on threats and the win condition.

Tech Stack

Gemini 3.1 Flash Live

Multimodal real-time AI for live video analysis and audio response generation over a persistent WebSocket connection.

FastAPI + WebSocket

Low-latency bidirectional communication between browser and AI pipeline, handling frame ingestion and audio forwarding.

OpenCV

Computer vision for loading screen detection using HSV color space analysis and region-based heuristics.

Web Audio API

Client-side PCM audio playback at 24kHz with buffered scheduling for seamless, gapless voice coaching.

Capabilities

80+ brawlers recognized with role, threat level, and counter-strategy data
4 game modes supported -- Gem Grab, Brawl Ball, Knockout, and Heist
Real-time threat assessment based on enemy team composition and proximity
Mode-specific strategy with coaching focused on win conditions and objectives
